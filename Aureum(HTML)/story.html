OUR HEART AND SOUL

Why Nefertum?

At Nefertum, we draw inspiration from the rich tapestry of ancient Egyptian mythology. Our name is an homage to Nefertum, a deity revered for his benevolence and captivating origin as a lotus flower emerging from primal waters. Much like the legend, Nefertum symbolizes the first rays of sunlight and embodies the sweet fragrance of the Egyptian blue lotus flower—an essence that echoes our commitment to crafting captivating perfumes.

Unveiling Extrait De Parfum

Dive into the realm of Extrait De Parfum, a pinnacle of olfactory luxury renowned for its extraordinary longevity, opulent quality, and unparalleled potency. This distinctive fragrance type boasts a concentration of fragrance compounds that is 2-3 times higher than the standard Eau de Parfum, ensuring a scent experience that lingers and projects powerfully. At Nefertum, we offer this exquisite essence to those seeking enduring, commanding fragrances that make a lasting impression.
