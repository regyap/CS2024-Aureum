PEAR & TONKA: A Symphony of Elegance and Joy

Within the opulent confines of "PEAR & TONKA" resides a harmonious symphony that speaks volumes in every olfactory note. At its inception, the lively dance between succulent Pears and the enigmatic allure of Blackcurrant sets the stage. The top notes orchestrate a jubilant overture, a melodic prelude that hints at the vibrancy within.

As this exquisite melody transitions to its heart, Violet takes center stage, infusing the composition with an ethereal elegance. Its presence, akin to a whisper of poetic romance, weaves an intricate tapestry of floral nuances, evoking an aura of timeless sophistication.

Yet, the true crescendo lies within the base notes. Here, Tonka and Vanilla intertwine, creating a sumptuous embrace that resonates deeply. Tonka's warm, nutty facets intertwine with the creamy sweetness of Vanilla, crafting an enchanting denouement that lingers as a memory, leaving an irresistible trail of allure.

Composed for those who seek not just attention but an unforgettable presence, "PEAR & TONKA" is a declaration of unapologetic elegance. It's more than a fragrance; it's an ode to the vivacious spirit and the unabashed celebration of life's exuberance.

TOP: Pear, Blackcurrant
MIDDLE: Violet
BASE: Tonka, Vanilla
INGREDIENTS: Alcohol Denat., fragrance, Citral, Coumarin, Geraniol, Linalool, BHT

VIOLET

Embodying elegance and grace, violet brings a touch of ethereal beauty to fragrances. Its floral and slightly powdery aroma carries an air of sophistication, conjuring visions of lush, blooming gardens. Violet introduces a charming and serene facet to scents, intertwining a sense of nostalgia and romance, reminiscent of soft petals carried by a gentle breeze.

PEAR

Like a succulent treasure from an orchard, pear infuses fragrances with a delicate, sweet nuance, evoking the sensation of biting into a ripe, juicy fruit. Its crisp and refreshing aura adds a luscious, fruity dimension that uplifts the entire scent profile, painting a vivid imagery of orchards in full bloom and embracing the essence of youthfulness and vitality.

BLACKCURRANT

Exuding an enchanting allure, blackcurrant infuses scents with its tantalizingly sweet and slightly tart profile. Its fruity and bold character creates a magnetic presence, adding depth and complexity to fragrances. Blackcurrant instills a sense of mystery and allure, painting a picture of dusky orchards laden with ripe, enticing berries, enhancing the overall allure of the composition.

With its bold yet refined demeanor, it captures the essence of joy and happiness, allowing every wearer to exude confidence and charisma effortlessly.

This Extrait De Parfum is not just a scent; it's an experience—a luxurious indulgence that encapsulates the allure of grand entrances and the subtlety of elegance, beckoning you to revel in its enchanting embrace.
