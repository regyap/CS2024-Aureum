WHITE MUSK & ROSE: Nefertum's Signature Elixir. Timeless Elegance and Captivating Mystery

Crafted as an ode to sophistication and allure, "White Musk & Rose" stands as the crown jewel in Nefertum's collection, a mesmerizing symphony that captures the essence of timeless beauty.

At its inception, the scent dances upon the skin with an enthralling overture. The luminous essence of Bergamot and the tangy embrace of Black Currant compose the invigorating top notes, akin to a sunlit garden in full bloom, inviting you into a world of resplendent fragrance.

Transitioning to its heart, this perfume delves into a sensuous embrace. The velvety petals of Rose and the rich depth of Tobacco entwine with the delicate whispers of Orange Blossom, creating a magnetic bouquet that evokes deep emotions and a sense of grandeur.

At its foundation, the fragrance rests upon an ethereal pedestal. The enigmatic allure of White Musk intertwines with the ancient charm of Olibanum, creating a sublime and lasting impression that weaves itself into the very fabric of your essence.

"White Musk & Rose" isn't merely a perfume; it's an embodiment of sophistication and enigma. It speaks a language of sensuality and empowerment, offering an olfactory experience that is both refined and seductive.

TOP: Bergamot, Blackcurrant
MIDDLE: Tobacco, Rose, Orange Blossom
BASE: White Musk, Olibanum
INGREDIENTS: Alcohol Denat. Citronella, Geraniol, Citral, Linalool, fragrance, Hydroxycitronellal, BHT

BERGAMOT

Like a burst of sunshine on a citrus orchard, bergamot brings a zesty, effervescent freshness to fragrances. Its vibrant and uplifting aroma captures the essence of Mediterranean summers, infusing scents with a sparkling and invigorating character. Bergamot adds a tantalizing citrusy touch that awakens the senses, painting an olfactory picture of sunny landscapes and crisp, breezy mornings.

ROSE

Embodied in the petals of romance, rose brings an exquisite and timeless elegance to scents. Its floral symphony evokes emotions of love and sophistication, enveloping fragrances in a delicate, intoxicating sweetness. Rose, with its multifaceted aroma, weaves a tale of passion and grace, reminiscent of blooming gardens and eternal romance.

PINK PEPPER

Adding a tantalizingly spicy twist, pink pepper infuses fragrances with a peppery, rosy warmth. Its vibrant and slightly fruity aroma offers a captivating contrast, lending a touch of enigmatic allure to scents. Pink pepper brings a subtle fiery essence, evoking images of exotic spice markets and adding a dash of excitement to the fragrance profile.

Embracing the skin, this signature elixir reveals a duality that is both timeless and modern. It exudes an irresistible smoky sweetness that dances between the realms of elegance and allurement, leaving an indelible mark on those it encounters.

Captivating, sophisticated, and daringly elegant, "White Musk & Rose" isn't just a fragrance; it's an experience crafted to celebrate the essence of allure and sophistication, inviting you to indulge in the seductive mysteries it unfurls.
