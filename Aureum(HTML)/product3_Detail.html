FRESH COTTON: Where Elegance Meets Comfort

Step into a world where sophistication intertwines seamlessly with comfort - that's the essence of Fresh Cotton. Imagine the first rays of dawn gently kissing your skin as you stroll through a field of freshly laundered linens. This fragrance is a manifestation of that serene morning embrace, capturing the essence of pure elegance and tranquil comfort.

At the heart of Fresh Cotton lies a delicate blend meticulously crafted to evoke an aura of pristine purity. The initial burst of aroma takes you on an olfactory journey, where the invigorating scent of citrus-infused alcohol dances playfully with the subtle yet unmistakable fragrance of freshly laundered linens. This symphony of notes unveils layers of complexity, each revealing a facet of its captivating personality.

The subtle nuances of Citronella and Geraniol add a refreshing zest, akin to a sun-kissed garden after a light summer rain. These elements interlace seamlessly with the gentle allure of a-Hexyl Cinnamic aldehyde and Alpha-iso-methylionone, creating an ethereal fragrance that lingers delicately in the air.

As the fragrance settles, the comforting embrace of Linalool and Eugenol emerges, reminiscent of a cozy afternoon wrapped in the softest cotton blanket. It is here that Fresh Cotton truly shines, offering a harmonious blend that is both unique and reassuringly familiar.

TOP: Orange, Cotton
MIDDLE: Muguet, Violet
BASE: Cedarwood, Amber, Musk
INGREDIENTS: Alcohol Denat, Fragrance, Citronella, Geraniol, a-Hexyl Cinnamic aldehyde, Alpha-iso-methylionone, Linalool. Eugenol, BHT

ORANGE

Orange exudes a lively burst of citrus vitality, akin to sun-kissed joy, infusing scents with vibrant freshness and an aura of optimism.

COTTON

Cotton whispers of pure comfort, evoking the tranquility of fresh laundry, offering a gentle, familiar embrace of serene cleanliness.

VIOLET

Violet's delicate allure brings refined sophistication, akin to strolling through a misty garden, leaving an enigmatic and elegantly floral impression.

Beyond its delightful aroma, Fresh Cotton exudes an undeniable sense of modern elegance. It encapsulates the zeitgeist of contemporary style, appealing to those with an affinity for the latest trends. Its clean, fruity floral notes evoke a sense of understated luxury, making it a statement piece for those who appreciate refinement.

Indulge in the allure of Fresh Cotton - a fragrance that not only captivates the senses but also embodies a modern ode to comfort, elegance, and the ever-evolving essence of style.
